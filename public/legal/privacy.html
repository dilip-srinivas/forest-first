Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026  ·  Forest First / The Heritage Foundation

Overview

Our Commitment

Forest First is an initiative of The Heritage Foundation, a registered public charitable trust based in Kotagiri, The Nilgiris, Tamil Nadu. We collect only what is necessary to operate our restoration programmes and communicate with supporters. We do not sell, rent, or trade personal data.

Data Collection

What We Collect

We may collect the following information when you interact with this site or contact us:

  • Name and email address (newsletter subscription, contact enquiries)
  • Organisation name and designation (institutional partnership enquiries)
  • General location (country/state, for regional reporting purposes only)
  • Browser and device type (via standard server logs, not linked to personal identity)

We do not collect payment information directly. Donations are processed through third-party platforms that maintain their own privacy policies.

Data Use

How We Use It

Information you provide is used solely to:

  • Send programme updates, field reports, and newsletters (if subscribed)
  • Respond to enquiries and partnership proposals
  • Comply with statutory reporting obligations as a registered trust
  • Improve site content and user experience in aggregate, anonymised form
Data Sharing

Third Parties

We do not share personal information with third parties except where required by law or where you have explicitly consented. Our site is hosted on Cloudflare Pages; standard Cloudflare infrastructure logs apply.

Your Rights

Access and Deletion

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal data we hold about you at any time. To make a request, write to us at dilip@the-heritage.org. We will respond within 30 days.
